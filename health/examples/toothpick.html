    About this study

    We know that it's much easier to stay healthy in some places than in others. Health is a result of so many factors - including whether we can build exercise into our daily lives (for example, by walking), whether it's easy to get out to green space, and whether we can access good health services like GPs, hospitals, and dentists in order to maintain our health. The map below shows our attempt to measure the places that are best and worst to live a healthy life. It takes multiple sources of open data, puts them together at a local authority level, and then ranks the areas according to these multiple factors. Hover over your area to see how it scores on all of these aspects - the best areas are marked dark pink, and the worst are marked white.
